OUR STORY

Aneeka' is a boutique from the northern part of Kerala to bring you the 'heaven of trendy styles and fashions' that you wish to have in your own wardrobes at your home, but surprisingly at affordable prices as well.

We mainly focus on bringing you different textile materials with unique design patterns with mind blowing color combinations without snatching your valets. We also bring you ready to stitch[semi-stitched]/ ready to wear designer garments according to your fashion tastes. And the most interesting fact about us is that we bring you all your fashion and style requirements and fashion desires directly into your home[By hand or by courier]. 'Cash On Delivery' [COD] service is available in selected cities. That is, you dont need to go to the street and wander along the streets for your product, instead we will come to you with the product you selected from our web site or Facebook page.
